What is SigDict?

LLipter, December 23, 2019

SigDict stands for signature dictionary

It's an application that helps people generate, store, manage, and share their files' digital signature.

It provides reliable services that improve security with no additional cost.

It's advanced, convenient, and most importantly, completely free of charge.
